# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 16, 2026_

EMBER is a personal health monitoring application that runs entirely on your own device.

## Information We Process

EMBER processes biometric and wellness data from your Oura Ring that you choose to share — including heart rate variability, sleep summaries, resting heart rate, and temperature trends — as well as optional check-in responses you enter manually.

## How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

- establish a personal baseline from your own historical readings,
- identify when current readings differ meaningfully from that baseline, and
- generate plain-language summaries to help you notice patterns over time.

Your data is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what you set it up to do.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your machine in the `data/` directory. It is not uploaded to any external server. The only external calls made are to the Oura API (to retrieve your ring data) and the Anthropic API (to generate your daily summary).

## Data Sharing

EMBER does not sell or share your personal data. No third parties have access to your biometric data or check-in responses.

## Data Security

Because data is stored locally, security depends on your own device. Keep your `.env` file private and do not commit it to version control.

## Your Choices

You control your data entirely. You can delete any file in `data/` at any time. To revoke Oura access, remove the app from your Oura developer account.
