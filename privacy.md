# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 16, 2026_

EMBER is designed to support early detection of mood state transitions using biometric trends.

## Information We Process

We may process continuous biometric and wellness data (for example: heart-rate variability, sleep stage summaries, resting heart rate, and temperature trends) that you choose to share.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- establish personalized baselines,
- detect significant physiological deviations, and
- provide insights related to potential mood-state changes.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We only share data with service providers or collaborators when needed to operate or improve EMBER, and subject to appropriate safeguards.

## Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data.

## Your Choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data, subject to applicable law and operational constraints.
