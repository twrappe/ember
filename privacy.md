# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-16

Sentinel does not sell personal data.

If you use Sentinel, we may process biometric and related health data that you choose to connect (for example, data from wearable integrations) to provide monitoring and alerting features.

## What we collect

- Account information (such as email address)
- Device and app usage data
- Data you explicitly connect or upload (including biometric/health-related data)

## How we use data

- To operate and improve Sentinel
- To provide support
- To maintain security and prevent abuse
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers that help us run Sentinel, under appropriate contractual protections, or when required by law.

## Your choices

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support.

## Contact

For privacy requests, contact: privacy@sentinel.app
